222 Slice(indices)

224 Represent an AbstractUnitRange of indices as a vector of the indices themselves.

226 Upon calling `to_indices()`, Colons are converted to Slice objects to represent
227 the indices over which the Colon spans. Slice objects are themselves unit
228 ranges with the same indices as those they wrap. This means that indexing into
229 Slice objects with an integer always returns that exact integer, and they
230 iterate over all the wrapped indices, even supporting offset indices.
